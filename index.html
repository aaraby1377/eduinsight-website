Eduinsight Smarter Tools, Better Grades

Eduinsight uses AI to analyze past papers and lecture content, uncover key patterns, and help students focus on what truly matters for better academic performance.

🌱About EduInsight

EduInsight is an AI powered learning platform built specifically for SLIIT students regarding DMS module. Developed by final year undergraduates at SLIIT, it integrates cutting edge technologies such as:

🤖

AI Based Content Analysis

The system analyzes past exam papers and lecture materials to identify important topics and question patterns. It helps generate model exam questions aligned with the syllabus to support effective exam preparation.

• Feature: Model Paper Generation

📚

Syllabus Aware Learning Support

The system provides study materials such as summaries, diagrams, and learning resources to help students better understand key concepts. It also supports students in completing assignments and improving their subject knowledge.

• Feature: CA Guidance

📊

Adaptive Evaluation & Feedback System

The system evaluates student answers and provides feedback to improve writing quality. It also tracks performance, identifies weak areas, and suggests additional practice to help students improve.

• Feature: Personalized Feedback & Adaptive Learning

🎯

Adaptive Personalization

The system analyzes student performance in quizzes to identify weak topics. Based on this, it provides personalized questions and study recommendations to support targeted learning.

• Feature: Adaptive Study Plans
